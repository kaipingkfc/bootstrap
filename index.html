Blue Lock

Japan had just suffered defeat at the 2018 FIFA World Cup. In desperate need of a win, the country realizes it needs an ace striker for its football team. A plan is put into motion to find and train the best striker possible. Three hundred of the best and brightest football players are then inducted into Blue Lock, a grueling training regimen that aims to decide who is strong enough to lead Japan to World Cup glory. Among those kicking the ball down the field is Yoichi Isagi, a player who is only somewhat convinced of his own skills and abilities.
